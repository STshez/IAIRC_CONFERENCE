INAUGURAL AFRICA INSURANCE
RESOLUTION CONFERENCE

Insurance Sector Resilience Beyond Failure: Resolution Policy & Practice

About The Event

This conference will bring together industry leaders, policymakers, and practitioners to explore strategies for building resilience, sharing innovative policies, and discussing practical solutions
for challenges facing the sector. The Inaugural Africa Insurance Resolution Conference marks
a milestone 20th anniversary for the Policyholders Compensation Fund. Join us in celebrating
two decades of service while addressing critical issues in the insurance sector.

Event Date

Monday to Wednesday
28-30 April 2025

Event Location

Celebrating 20 Years of Excellence
